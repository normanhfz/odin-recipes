Instant Noodle

Description

Instant noodles are eaten when you are hungry and you are too lazy to get food from outside. Its easy to cook, flavourful, delicious and could last you awhile before you get hungry again

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Cup Noodles

Steps

  1. Boil 500 ml of water
  2. Open all condiments and sauces into the cup noodle of your choice
  3. Pour boiled water into the cup noodles
  4. Waitfor 5 minutes or not. I'm not your boss
  5. Noodles are ready to be enjoyed
