Peanut butter sandwich

Description

A sandwich that taste superb and can be eaten anytime

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Peanut butter
  • Jam

Steps

  1. Spread the peanut butter on a slice of bread
  2. Spread the jam on another slice of bread
  3. Combine the breads making the spreads meet in the middle
  4. The sandwich is ready to be eaten or can be toasted for extra goodness
  5. Enjoy
